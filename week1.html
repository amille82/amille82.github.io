Favorite Website: Week 1

Electric Mainline


For this first homework assignment, I chose the website "Electric Mainline." Electric Mainline serves as Creative Art Director Leigh Whipday's online portfolio of his works and projects.

What I liked about his website was the sectioned/split layout created with the change of background color for each different section. I've seen this style used on other websites before and I really like the way it divides content. I also love the use of anchored links for easy page-navigation and think those two different features work well together.
